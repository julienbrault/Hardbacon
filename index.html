About Us.

Founded by former business journalist Julien Brault, Hardbacon is a startup dedicated to helping the world’s 7 billion people to invest. Through smart tools, apps and great content, our goal is to educate people about investing, simplify their decision process and provide them with exclusive investment opportunities. We currently operates exclusively in Canada, but plan to expand in other countries shortly. If you would like to help us execute on our mission, drop us an email at : career@hardbacon.ca
